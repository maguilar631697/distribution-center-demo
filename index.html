Built around the real workflow.

Instead of a loose Google Form and a separate payment link, the system keeps the customer, the distribution center, and the back office in one controlled process.

1

Customer order

The customer chooses a location, products, quantities, pickup date, and contact information from a guided responsive form.

2

Deposit payment

The portal calculates a required deposit and sends the customer to a payment checkout with the order reference attached.

3

Pickup completion

Staff sees the order status, verifies inventory, collects final payment, and marks the order complete at pickup.
